Examples

Hagrid differs from other libraries only in the way that the styles are accessed, so a lot of what you see with other grid frameworks will translate directly to your usage here.

Hagrid supports nested rows. So if, for example, you need to break a 75% span down into thirds, you shouldn't have a problem.

Main Column with sidebar

<div class="example content" data-hagrid="grid-container">
    <!-- Rows -->
    <div class="collection" data-hagrid="grid-row">
        <!-- Columns/spans -->
        <div data-hagrid="span-33">
            <div class="example-block">

            </div>
        </div>
        <div data-hagrid="span-66">
            <div class="example-block">

            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
