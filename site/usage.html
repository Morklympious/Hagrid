Usage

Okay, there are three main hagrid attributes you'll need for building grids: 

 data-hagrid="grid-container" 
 data-hagrid="grid-row" 
 data-hagrid="span-X"

Here's how you set it up: 

<div class="example content" data-hagrid="grid-container">
    <!-- Rows -->
</div>

As you can see, the beginnings of our grid are made with data attributes, from here we can add rows to our container. A grid container may contain any number of grid rows. 

<div class="example content" data-hagrid="grid-container">
    <!-- Rows -->
    <div class="collection" data-hagrid="grid-row">
        <!-- Columns/spans -->
    </div>

    <div class="collection" data-hagrid="grid-row">
        <!-- Columns/spans -->
    </div>

    <div class="collection" data-hagrid="grid-row">
        <!-- Columns/spans -->
    </div>
</div>

Let's throw down the data attributes to make columns! 

<div class="example content" data-hagrid="grid-container">
    <!-- Rows -->
    <div class="collection" data-hagrid="grid-row">
        <!-- Columns/spans -->
        <div data-hagrid="span-25">
            <div class="example-block">

            </div>
        </div>
        <div data-hagrid="span-75">
            <div class="example-block">

            </div>
        </div>
    </div>

    <div class="collection" data-hagrid="grid-row">
        <!-- Columns/spans -->
        <div data-hagrid="span-75">
            <div class="example-block">

            </div>
        </div>
        <div data-hagrid="span-25">
            <div class="example-block">

            </div>
        </div>

    </div>

    <div class="collection" data-hagrid="grid-row">
        <!-- Columns/spans -->
        <div data-hagrid="span-50">
            <div class="example-block">

            </div>
        </div>
        <div data-hagrid="span-50">
            <div class="example-block">

            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
